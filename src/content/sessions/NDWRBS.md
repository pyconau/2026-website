abstract: "Between early 2025 and April 2026, peak weekday PR merges to Honeycomb's
  monorepo went from about 30 to about 74. Most of the delta came in the form of PRs
  that we could prove AI touched. This talk is the honest report-out from eighteen
  months of AI-assisted engineering: the calibration behind that headline number,
  the overlapping factors we can name but can't cleanly isolate, and the practices
  that made throughput compound without wrecking things underneath.\r\n\r\nAI amplifies
  your existing practices. A dysfunctional org gets more dysfunctional; one that already
  has autonomy, ownership, and feedback loops gets faster without breaking. Which
  shape you land in is downstream of the substrate you already have, not the models
  you can access."
