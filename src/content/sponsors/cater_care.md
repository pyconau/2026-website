Established in 1999, Cater Care stands as a pioneer in the food and beverage industry, managing more than 280 sites and 3,000 employees nationally. Supporting this footprint is an innovative internal tech team managing the technology backend that connects the paddock to the plate across childcare, education, corporate, remote, retail and aged care sectors. We are proud to use, advocate for, and give back to the open-source technology powering our modern enterprise architecture—from our managed procurement software (Buylink) to early-learning nutrition (Mini Meals), education catering (gather), and texture-modified texturing solutions (Care Food Co.).
