The Girls' Programming Network is a free program that fosters communities of girls, women, and gender-diverse people around Australia to encourage and expand their love of coding and technology. From year three, through high school, to university and beyond into the tech industry, GPN provides support, encouragement and opportunities for under-represented people to learn to code, build technical confidence, and develop leadership skills – all whilst growing a network of like-minded peers and mentors.
