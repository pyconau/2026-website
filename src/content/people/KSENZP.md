[Kattni](https://kattni.com) (she/her) is a creator, maker, photographer, programmer, classically trained vocalist, intermittent chef, air plant cultivator, casual knitter, and fledgling synth nerd. She is passionate about learning new things, and sharing her knowledge in an approachable way. She is tolerated by a cat and three kittens who continue to let her live with them.

She is a core development team member with the [BeeWare project](https://beeware.org), where she focusses on improving the documentation and information architecture. She has orchestrated a complete shift of the documentation backend from Sphinx to MkDocs, as well as moving the website from Lektor to MkDocs. She is working on building a community around the project by, among other things, making contributing more approachable through various changes in their contribution process.
