Justin is the founder and principal consultant at PivotNine, a boutique analyst and consulting firm based in Melbourne, Australia. He covers enterprise infrastructure, cloud, and information security technologies with a particular focus on free and open source software. An IT industry veteran with extensive global experience, Justin has worked with lots of enterprises and startups you've definitely heard of, and plenty that you almost certainly haven't.



Justin holds an MBA from Melbourne Business School, and is a graduate member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors. He lives in Melbourne, Australia with his human family, and three chickens.
