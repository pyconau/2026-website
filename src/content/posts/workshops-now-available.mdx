Thats right everyone! Workshop registrations for PyCon AU 2026 are now open!

We're excited to announce three workshops running on 26 August 2026. Workshops run for approximately 2 hours each, and are sit-down instructor led deep-dives. In 2026 we're excited to host workshops run by both sponsors (Meta &amp; Temporal) and the PyCon AU community.

Workshops are free to attend for all conference delegates, but due to capacity constraints they require specific, seperate registration. All new tickets sold will be offered to add workshops on the second step in Pretix. Existing attendees can add workshops by clicking the "Change Order" button on your order.

If you would like to attend just the workshops (and not the rest of the conference) you can purchase a "Workshops only" ticket for $150. This includes access to all workshops on Wednesday 26 August, but not the rest of PyCon AU.
